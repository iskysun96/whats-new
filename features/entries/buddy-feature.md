## What it does
The Buddy feature lets you hatch a small virtual creature that lives in your Claude Code interface and watches you code. Your buddy reacts to your coding activities, grows over time, and provides gentle companionship during those long debugging sessions. It's a playful addition that adds personality to your development environment.

## When to use it
- When you want a fun, lighthearted coding companion during solo development sessions
- To gamify your coding experience and make long work sessions more engaging
- When you're feeling isolated while coding and want some virtual company
- To celebrate milestones in your code with a buddy that reacts to your progress
- During hackathons or coding challenges to add an element of fun

## How to use it
1. Type `/buddy` in any chat or command interface
2. Choose your buddy type from the selection menu that appears
3. Give your buddy a name when prompted
4. Watch as your buddy hatches and begins observing your coding session
5. Continue coding normally - your buddy will react to file saves, successful builds, errors, and other coding events
6. Check your buddy's status and mood by hovering over it or typing `/buddy status`

## Pro tips
- Your buddy's happiness increases with successful commits and decreases with build errors - keep it happy with clean code!
- Different buddy types have unique personalities and reactions, so experiment to find your favorite
- Your buddy remembers your coding patterns over time and will develop preferences for certain languages or activities
